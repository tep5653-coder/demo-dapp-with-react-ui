RiskTowerBot Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-12

This Privacy Policy explains how RiskTowerBot ("we", "our", or "us") may collect, use, and process limited technical and interaction data when you use the Service.

1. Scope
This Privacy Policy applies to the RiskTowerBot application, Telegram Mini App interfaces, related websites, and technical infrastructure used to operate the Service.

2. Data We May Process
Depending on how the Service is configured, we may process:
- wallet address information after connection;
- technical session identifiers;
- device and browser-related technical information;
- Telegram Mini App context data made available by the Telegram platform;
- interaction events within the application interface;
- transaction-related metadata visible through public blockchain infrastructure.

3. Data We Do Not Store
We do not store:
- seed phrases;
- wallet private keys;
- wallet recovery phrases.

4. Wallet Providers and Blockchain Networks
Wallet connection and transaction approval functions are provided through third-party wallet software and blockchain infrastructure.
Public blockchain transactions may be permanently visible on the blockchain.

5. How We Use Data
We may use technical and interaction data to:
- operate the Service;
- maintain sessions;
- improve stability and performance;
- troubleshoot technical issues;
- detect abuse, fraud, or misuse;
- maintain security.

6. Data Sharing
We may share limited technical data with infrastructure, hosting, analytics, wallet integration, or security providers only to the extent reasonably necessary to operate or protect the Service.

7. Data Retention
We may retain technical logs, interaction records, and operational data for as long as reasonably necessary for security, troubleshooting, compliance, and service operation purposes.

8. Security
We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data handled by the Service.
However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. International Use
You are responsible for determining whether your use of the Service is lawful in your jurisdiction.

10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
The latest version will be made available through the Service or related interfaces.

11. Contact
For privacy-related questions, use the official contact channel associated with RiskTowerBot.
